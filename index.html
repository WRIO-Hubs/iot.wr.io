A Web 3.0 IoT Platform

We are giving our world a digital nervous system: motion, temperature, humidity and other types of sensors and devices. The Internet of Things will become a bridge that joins the physical and electronic realms. The project’s goal is to become a homepage for every IoT device and sensor. And to provide a platform for the development of 3rd party IoT services based on the global database of smart devices. Anyone will be able to register their devices absolutely free, allowing them to configure, monitor and manage their own IoT networks. The global public database of devices created by the community will store information about devices from the stage of development through recycling: manufacturer, date of manufacture; seller, selling time and locations; warranty service; usage, errors and maintenance logs; etc.




Mission

Develop an open-source, decentralized and secure Facebook for smart devices and sensors.

Problem Problem

There are 23 billion connected devices in the world; this number doubles every 36 months and will exceed 100 billion in the next decade. All these autonomous sensors require secure auto management and tracking. The information will allow optimization when designing smart networks and the reduction of related operation and maintenance costs. It shall be noted that machines begin interacting with each other and become market participants: they purchase and sell services. E.g. smart wind turbines or solar panels and street lighting, automatic charging pods and drones, etc.

Facts


Lack of security

Due to technical peculiarities, the IoT sector is the most vulnerable to hackers’ attacks, and reading about botnets consisting of thousands of devices becomes a common think. E.g. botnet Mirai consisting of hundred thousands of IP-cameras

Inefficient resource management

Poor management of electric power, water and heat result in their overconsumption in certain cases up to 50%

Absence of automatic traffic and infrastructure control

In downtown San Francisco 20-30% of all traffic congestion is caused by people hunting for a parking spot

Overwhelming number of IoT devices

Separated networks of smart homes, manufactures, smart cities, etc. can contain from a dozen to dozens of millions of devices and sensors. They need automatic and efficient control with minimal human participation

Solution

The solution is based on a machine-readable web 3.0 platform — WRIO Internet OS developed by our team.
Every device gets a unique ID linked to the owner’s address, thus enabling an authorized control over the device. The user and device profiles in machine-readable key:value data format are API-free and accessed through HTTP without vendor lock-in. Such format enables automatic data processing and creates new business opportunities. We will witness unique IoT services based on open, shareable, reusable, semantic and decentralized IoT database. Semantics enables machines to “understand” pieces of data and how they are interrelated, which is the basis for machine-learning and fuzzy logic algorithms. Such algorithms will allow machines to create complex logic patterns for processing IoT Big Data without any need in direct human programming, and to detect optimal solutions without walking through all possible options, thus making it a machine analogue of intuition.

Business model Business model

Our approach is to create a unified IoT network and public database of smart devices with the provision of their data in the machine-readable format. This enables automated collection and processing of all kinds of statistics, which will be used to develop data-driven services and business decisions. It is hard to overestimate the value of such combined big data of millions and soon trillions of devices for developers, business, smart cities, owners of smart stores/offices/homes, etc. where every new smart device add synergy. Our business model is similar to other social media platforms like LinkedIn or Facebook. It relies on the sale of premium accounts with an access to advanced features, tools and statistics, on receiving a percentage of sold data, 3rd party services and payments between IoT devices. Highly targeted ads and promotion services also will be a source of income.

Our clients


IoT owners

IoT owners will be able to consolidate into networks. Unlike people, most of devices do not have to protect their data, and owners can get revenue by selling their data

IoT manufacturers

Business

Roadmap roadmap

Minimum viable product
  • development of a blockchain-based authentication module
  • blockchain registration of IoT devices
  • create and display IoT profiles as web pages based on schema.org.
    Check Tech Demo.
  • subscription and notification functions
  • development of paid functions to control devices and access to their premium data and statistics
Initial database

Before the project will be completed we plan to develop a MVP, conduct first sales, and get enough traction to attract investors. Participation in EU programmes will help us to solve a chicken-egg issue: we will create the initial IoT database of thousands of devices with the help of EU testbeds (SmartSantander and dozen others). This will help us demonstrate the main idea and benefits of commercial use to the first clients. First partner-manufacturers will get free premium access to statistics.

Promotion

As one of the first promotion techniques we give an opportunity to manage devices via commands in social media and to display selected alerts and notifications in the feed, so that subscribers could see them. Every message will contain a link to our platform. Referral campaign will allow our early adopters attract new users for rewards and free access to premium features.
